Detailed Bio

I am Abhisek Mishra, a data science enthusiast with a strong academic foundation and practical expertise in solving complex challenges through technology. I earned my Integrated MCA from Utkal University, Bhubaneswar, where I honed my skills in programming, data analysis, and machine learning.

During my tenure as a Data Science Intern at Fightright Technologies, I worked on automating and optimizing data-driven processes. My responsibilities included maintaining data pipelines for scraping district court websites using Selenium WebDriver and securely storing the results in Amazon S3 for efficient data extraction. I also designed and implemented OCR processes by developing AWS Lambda functions integrated with Docker and Amazon ECR, enabling automated extraction and organization of text from image-based legal documents. Additionally, I gained hands-on experience with MySQL, performing CRUD operations, data cleaning, and validation.

My academic journey also includes significant projects in Machine Learning, such as an Email Spam Classifier, Car Price Prediction, and Audio Classification. Beyond ML, I have explored web development and GUI-based applications, building tools like a Web Scraper, Weather App, and Password Generator.

With a passion for technology and problem-solving, I am eager to apply my skills to innovative projects and drive meaningful impact in the field of data science.
